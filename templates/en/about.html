About Robo‐Study Inc.

Robo‐Study Inc. was founded in October 2018 in Hamamatsu City, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan.
Our mission is to leverage generative AI to help prevent loneliness among the elderly,
and we develop and sell AI communication robots such as AI‐Mimakun for elder care monitoring.

Business Activities

  • Planning, development, and sales of AI communication robots (e.g., elder monitoring robot “AI‐Mimakun”)
  • Creation and customization of AI chatbot solutions
  • Planning and provision of AI learning materials (for technical high schools, universities, and companies)
  • Personal history (“Jibunshi”) creation programs and lifelong learning support for seniors

Mission

In Japan’s rapidly aging society, preventing isolation and loneliness among seniors is a critical challenge.
Our goal is to integrate AI and robotics to support elder monitoring and communication,
enabling seniors to continue living with dignity in their communities.
We also create AI-driven educational tools to inspire the next generation of engineers and AI practitioners.
